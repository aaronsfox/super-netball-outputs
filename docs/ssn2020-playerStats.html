Suncorp Super Netball 2020: Player Metrics

Performance insights by Deakin’s Centre for Sport Research. Please feel free to contact the author of this work, Aaron Fox (aaron.f@deakin.edu.au), if you have any questions about the analyses.

The following tables provide summary metrics for players from Suncorp Super Netball 2020. Metrics are split into loose attacking and defensive categories.

Attacking metrics include:

  • Centre Pass Receives
  • Circle Feeds
  • Circle Feeds with Shot Attempts
  • Goal Assists
  • General Play Turnovers
  • Intercept Passes Thrown

Defensive metrics include:

  • Gains
  • Deflections
  • Deflections with Gain
  • Deflections with No Gain
  • Contact Penalties
  • Obstruction Penalties

Suncorp Super Netball 2020: Player Metrics (Attacking)

Player metrics for attacking categories in Suncorp Super Netball 2020. Within each table, both absolute totals and per 15 minute totals (i.e. metric total relative to 15 minutes played) are presented. Players must have been on-court a minimum of 15 minutes to be included. Team (e.g. 'Vixens') or player (e.g. 'Watson') names can be looked up using the search bars.
